Blistered Cherry Tomatoes

Description:

Quick and easy, Sweet and Bright!
Made with as little as 2 ingredients!
There's no way to mess this up!

Ingredients

  • 1 Pint of tomato/grape tomatoes
  • 1/4 cup Olive oil

Optional

  • 4 Minced Garlic Cloves
  • 1/4 Cup Chopped White Onion
  • Add to taste: salt, pepper, sugar, oregano, thyme and whatever other spices you personally enjoy!
  • Fresh Basil Leaves

Steps:

  1. Heat oil in a small pot or saucepan
  2. Once oil is hot, add Tomatoes and stir until covered
  3. Bring to light simmer, stiring from time to time until all tomatoes are blistered!

If adding garlic or white onion, add before step 2 then proceed

Add any spices into step 3

Add fresh basil on top

Pairs well with any pasta!
